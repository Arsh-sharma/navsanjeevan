Women Awareness Programme

The Trust has organized various programs on women’s rights in the district. This program is attended by SHG members or women participants.

Youth Development

Skill Education Navsanjeevan Planned to work with youth . We have started a vocational training program at the school level in the Pune district with 6th to 12th std with affiliation with DEVT and NSDC.This initivative was one the many initiatives.

Women Empowerment

Self-help groups Navsanjeevan social trust works with women for the overall development of women through SHG's since 2013. A total of 17 villages and 230 Self-help groups were formed and training was provided to 2500 women. Navsanjeevan social trust works in close collaboration with the Government department.
